The Standing Wave

About

The Standing Wave is a publication about self-sustaining systems — the loops, cycles, and standing patterns that hold their shape while everything inside them flows through and leaves. A flame. A sourdough starter. A language. An economy. A sealed terrarium. The wave a river makes over a rock.

Each issue takes one such system and explains it — beautifully, and accurately. The aim is the quiet jolt of understanding something true about the world: that many of the most enduring things around us are not solid objects at all, but patterns that matter and energy pass through.

Issues are short, evergreen, and arrive every few days. Accuracy is the whole point: claims are checked against current sources, and those sources are listed at the foot of every piece. If something can’t be said well and truthfully, it doesn’t ship.

Written by Mark Matthews.
